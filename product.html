Anti-cheating & Proctoring (20+ measures)

  • Identity: face detection, photo ID verification, live face/selfie match, biometric verification, AI image analysis against submitted ID.
  • Environment: dual-camera proctoring (phone + laptop room scan), snapshot proctoring at intervals, live environment check, room scan, object detection.
  • Browser lockdown: forced full-screen mode, tab proctoring, copy/paste tracking, app/monitor blocking, keyboard-shortcut restrictions, no-exit lockdown — built into the test flow with no extra installation.
  • AI monitoring: AI-assistance / GPT-text detection, gaze/eye-movement tracking, keystroke dynamics, talking-prohibition alerts, typing-pattern baselines, Focus Guard.
  • Session evidence: full session and screen recording, webcam snapshots, IP and geolocation tracking (blocks VPN masking), question-level activity logs, timestamped incident reports.
  • Question integrity: randomized question banks and answer order, honesty agreements, private invites.
  • Configurable: Standard / Strict / Custom security modes; set violation tolerances, snapshot intervals, and warn/flag/terminate thresholds.
  • Four proctoring models: automated/AI, live (human), record-and-review, and hybrid — switchable per exam.
  • AI bias handling: model trained for poor lighting and darker skin tones.

Common Questions & Objections

Q: Can I build a custom assessment, or do I have to use pre-built tests?
A: Both. Choose from 3,500+ validated tests, build your own, or combine the two — with 25+ question formats including video, audio, coding, Google/Microsoft simulations, and fill-in-the-blank. AI can draft a role-specific assessment for you from a few inputs.

Q: How does Testlify stop candidates from cheating on remote assessments?
A: 20+ built-in proctoring measures — Focus Guard, full-screen enforcement, tab-switch and copy-paste detection, dual-camera, webcam snapshots, IP/location verification, AI-assistance detection, and optional live proctoring — with an activity log on every assessment.

Q: Does Testlify integrate with the ATS my team already uses?
A: Most likely. Testlify connects natively with 100+ ATS and HRMS platforms — Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, Greenhouse, Lever, UKG, and more — with no middleware or data mapping. Scores, reports, and hiring statuses sync automatically.

Q: How does AI scoring work, and can I trust it?
A: Testlify auto-evaluates video, audio, chat, and coding responses in real time and returns skill-level insights, benchmark comparisons, and candidate summaries. AI assists the evaluation; recruiters keep control of the final decision and can override any score.

Q: Is Testlify compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other data-privacy standards?
A: Yes — ISO 27001 certified, SOC 2 Type II compliant, GDPR and CCPA supported, with role-based access controls and configurable data-retention policies (plus FERPA and ISO 23988 for proctoring, and NYC Local Law 144 audit logs).

Q: How does Testlify's pricing work?
A: Credit-based — you spend a credit only when an invited candidate starts an assessment. Pay monthly or annually, lock your base price for 24 months, and cancel anytime. A 7-day free trial (no card) and a 30-day money-back guarantee keep it risk-free.

Q: Can candidates take assessments on mobile, and how long do they take?
A: Yes — any modern browser on desktop, tablet, or mobile, no install required. Most assessments are designed to take under 45 minutes (often ~30), built for high completion rates.

Q: Can we white-label assessments with our own branding?
A: Yes. Remove Testlify branding, use a custom domain and your own email infrastructure, and deliver a branded candidate portal so candidates experience your brand from invite to offer.

Content Creation Guidelines

  1. Lead with benefits, not features — open with the outcome (55% faster time-to-hire), then the mechanism (AI instant scoring).
  2. Use specific examples — name the role, the test, or the number; "assess a rig technician's HSE knowledge" beats "assess candidates."
  3. Include proof points — reach for a real stat (1,500+ teams, 3,500+ tests, SOC 2 / ISO 27001, G2 4.7) before a superlative.
  4. Address objections proactively — name the concern (cheating, bias, lock-in, budget) and answer it with the matching real capability.
